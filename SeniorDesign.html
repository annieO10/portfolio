Recreation Program Trip Planning and Management System

Our client for this project is the recreation center at Iowa State. They need a way to keep track of important imformation for student trips. This website would allow students to signup for trips at the recreation center, keep track of waivers, medical information, dietary restrictions, emergency contact, and names of trips goers, and it would allow trip leaders to easily communicate information to trip goers.

My Role and Expectations

I am the UI/UX design lead for this project. Meaning I mostly work on the frontend to create pages, get information from backend, and plan different functions for this website. I am excited to get experience working with a team on a project this large. I am also looking forward to expanding my web development skills and getting more creative with this project. By the end of the project I hope to see all the functions I have put forward to the group being developed. I also hope I help to create an interesting and engaging design for the UI.
